Time integrator

For the time discretization we have adopted the elegant solution which was found by Rottler and Maggs [1] and allows to conserve both time-reversibility and phase-space volume conservation:

  1. Update the particle momenta by half a time step.
  2. Update the field by half a time step.
  3. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  4. Update the electric field in direction by half a time step.
  5. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  6. Update the electric field in direction by half a time step.
  7. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  8. Update the electric field in direction by a full time step.
  9. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  10. Update the electric field in direction by half a time step.
  11. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  12. Update the electric field in direction by half a time step.
  13. Update the particle positions in direction by half a time step.
  14. Update the field by half a time step.
  15. Update the particle momenta by half a time step.
