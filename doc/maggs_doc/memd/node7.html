In our algorithm we use Yukawa screening to resolve interparticle interactions on a local scale, such that larger spacings are feasible. In the method we subtract the self-energy for both the screened and the unscreened interaction separately by the respective exact lattice Green's function. Finally, the Yukawa potential is corrected at short distances by adding an extra analytic Yukawa potential (with opposite sign) to the truncated Lennard-Jones potential.
