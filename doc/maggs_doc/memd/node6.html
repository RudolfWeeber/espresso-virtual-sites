Self-energy

The interpolation of the charges onto the lattice gives rise to the artificial force exerted on the particle by its own field. In order to cure this remedy, the direct subtraction of the self-energy is introduced.

For the interpolated charge cloud the self-energy can be directly calculated (see Ref.[2]). For the simple cubic lattice in three dimensions the linear interpolation will give 8 charges which are placed at the corners of the cube with edge length (see Fig. ).
